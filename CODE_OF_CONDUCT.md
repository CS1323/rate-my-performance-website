# Code of Conduct

Rate My Performance CFU is committed to a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful environment for all contributors and community members.

---

## Our Values

- **Respect** — treat everyone with kindness and professionalism
- **Inclusivity** — welcome people of all backgrounds and identities
- **Transparency** — communicate openly and honestly
- **Accountability** — take responsibility for your actions and words

---

## Expected Behaviour

- Use welcoming, inclusive language
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully
- Prioritise what is best for the community
- Show empathy towards others

---

## Unacceptable Behaviour

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory comments based on race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, religion, disability, or any protected characteristic
- Sharing others' personal information without consent (doxxing)
- Sexual harassment or inappropriate sexual content
- Deliberate trolling or sustained disruptive behaviour
- Hate speech or content promoting violence

---

## Reporting

**On the website** — use the **Report button** on any comment.

**In GitHub discussions / PRs** — contact a maintainer privately:

Email: **cadence@cadencekeys.com**  
Subject: `[CoC Report] <brief description>`

Include what happened, when, who was involved, and any supporting links or screenshots. All reports are kept confidential.

---

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including:

- Private warning
- Temporary suspension from contributions
- Permanent ban for severe or repeated violations

Appeals can be sent to cadence@cadencekeys.com with `[CoC Appeal]` in the subject line.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces (GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions) and to interactions in any related community channels.
